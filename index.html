Executive overview

Signal Mesh treats every industrial data exchange as a contract with explicit schema, freshness, quality, lineage, and ownership obligations. Contracts are versioned as code, enforced in real time, and audited across the enterprise.

Contractual operational cognition

Every stream is an agreement. We validate structure, temporal behavior, and failure vectors continuously so operational intent stays explicit and enforceable.

Temporal awareness

Trace violations back to the exact moment they emerged. Time scrubber tooling rewinds the entire contract landscape for forensic clarity.

Federated stewardship

Git-native workflows unify plant teams, data engineers, and compliance into a shared contract lifecycle with review gates and cryptographic approval.

Metadata-first control plane

We ingest metadata, not payloads. This preserves OT safety, reduces bandwidth, and delivers deep operational context without touching production data.

Operational governance cockpit

A dense, time-aware workspace for monitoring contract health, resolving incidents, and coordinating cross-functional teams under pressure.
